About

Stories, guides, and practical advice from our writers on tech, design, and community events.

Our mission

We publish clear, helpful content to connect people with events, ideas, and each other. Whether you're a reader looking for inspiration or an organizer wanting to reach a wider audience, our goal is to make knowledge accessible and useful.

Our story

Started by a small team of event-curators and writers, this blog grew from weekend meetups and a shared passion for thoughtful, practical content. Over time we've expanded our coverage to include tutorials, event spotlights, and interviews with community leaders.

What we cover

  • Event highlights and previews
  • How-to guides for organizers and attendees
  • Interviews with practitioners and community members
